All of the data stored by digital devices is encoded as bits. A group of 8 bits is called byte.
We discussed several types of data representations during this class for computers. 
One way data can be represented is binary system. This is a way of representing data using 0s and 1s.
For reperesenting text ASCII or Unicode systems are employed by digital devices.
